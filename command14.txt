Experiment 14: 
Write a shell script to input a number and display its factorial.

file2.sh
#!/bin/bash
read -p "Enter a number: " n
fact=1
for (( i=1; i<=n; i++ ))
do
    fact=$((fact * i))
done
echo "Factorial of $n is $fact"
